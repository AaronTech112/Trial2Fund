How long does it take to get funded after passing the challenge?

We guarantee propfirm account purchase within 48 hours of successfully passing our trial challenge.

Are there any hidden rules or requirements?

No, we pride ourselves on transparency. There are no consistency rules or hidden requirements. Our simple rules are designed to help traders succeed.
