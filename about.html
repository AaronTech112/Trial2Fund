About Trial2Fund

Empowering traders worldwide with affordable access to proprietary trading opportunities

Our Story

Trial2Fund was founded in 2022 by a team of passionate traders and fintech entrepreneurs who recognized a significant barrier in the trading industry: the high cost of entry to proprietary trading firms.

We observed that many skilled traders, particularly in emerging markets, were unable to access the capital they needed to maximize their trading potential due to the expensive evaluation fees charged by prop firms.

Our solution was simple yet revolutionary: create an affordable pathway for talented traders to access prop firm accounts by offering low-cost trial challenges that mirror prop firm evaluations. When traders succeed, we purchase real prop firm accounts for them, creating a win-win scenario.

Today, Trial2Fund has helped hundreds of traders worldwide access professional trading capital, with a particular focus on supporting traders in regions where traditional prop firm fees are prohibitively expensive.
