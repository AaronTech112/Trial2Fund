About Trial2Trade

Bridge to Prop Firm Funding

Our Story

Trial2Trade was born with a clear mission: to give talented traders access to prop firm accounts without the financial barriers.

We independently source accounts from prop firms — FundedNext, The5ers, FTMO, and FundingPips — so that traders who prove their skills can step into real opportunities and real capital.

It all began on May 19th, 2025, with just a bold vision: To remove the financial obstacles that hold back talented, up-and-coming traders — especially in Africa.

We saw the hustle.
We saw the talent.
But most of all, we saw the struggle.

Too many skilled traders were blocked not by their ability, but by the cost of prop firm challenges. So we decided to change the rules.

Instead of paying hundreds — sometimes thousands — of dollars to prove their skills, traders can now get a Trial2Trade challenge account at a fraction of the price.
And if they pass? We source them with a full prop firm account.

In just a few months, Trial2Trade has proudly helped over 100+ traders enter the big leagues, sourcing over $900,000 worth of prop firm accounts for rising talent across the continent.

This is more than a platform. It’s a community and a pathway for skillful, determined traders to grow, learn, and access capital without taking unnecessary financial risks.

About the Founder

Evalsam Idor

Founder & CEO, Trial2Trade

Evalsam Idor started Trial2Trade with a single goal: to remove financial barriers for talented traders and give them access to real prop firm capital.

With a strong background in forex trading, Evalsam understood firsthand the challenges skilled traders face — not lack of talent, but lack of opportunity. He decided to create a platform that empowers upcoming traders to step into the big leagues without unnecessary financial risk.

While Evalsam is the public face and driving force behind Trial2Trade, he acknowledges that the journey would not have been possible without the support of other dedicated co-founders and partners, who help manage operations, partnerships, and the platform’s day-to-day growth.

Together, this team ensures that Trial2Trade is not just a service, but a trusted community for skilled, motivated traders to learn, grow, and access funded accounts.
